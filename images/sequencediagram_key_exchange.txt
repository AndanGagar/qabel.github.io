Bob -> Contacts: New Contact

Alice <- Util: ScanQRCode()

Crypto --> Util: decrypted contact data
Util --> Contacts: contact data
Contacts -->  Bob: Shows contact data
Bob -> Contacts: SaveContact(Data)
Alice -> Bob: do the same stuff
